How it works

Simple, transparent, community-led

1

Join your community

Register with your organisation's 121.ai platform. Every member is a verified peer within the same closed community — no strangers.

2

Get assessed or deposit

Borrowers complete a credibility assessment and receive a transparent score. Lenders deposit funds into their platform balance and start browsing.

3

Lend, borrow, repay

Lenders browse verified borrowers and make offers on their terms. Borrowers accept and repay on a clear schedule. Every step recorded.

Why closed?

Trust bounded by community

Unlike open P2P marketplaces, 121.ai only connects people who already share a common bond — colleagues, classmates, co-op members. This shared context reduces risk, increases accountability, and makes every transaction more meaningful.

Bigger picture

Building financial identities for the invisible

Millions of people — international students, immigrants, first-time earners — are financially capable but financially invisible. They have no credit history, so they get no loans. 121.ai changes that, one community at a time.

For Individuals

A dignified path into the financial system

Every repayment made is a data point that builds a verifiable financial identity — opening doors that were previously closed regardless of character or capability.

For Governments

Real credit data for invisible populations

Granular credit behaviour data for populations absent from national registers — supporting evidence-based policy for financial inclusion and housing.

For Banks

A trusted onboarding pathway

121.ai credibility records serve as a first signal of reliability — a structured bridge between the informal and formal financial world.
